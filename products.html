Loreal Matrix


Matrix is a professional hair care and hair color brand owned by L'Oréal. It is part of L'Oréal's Professional Products Division. Founded in 1980 by Arnie and Sydell Miller, Matrix aims to build a global community of hairdressers and provide them with products and services to express themselves and inspire others.

Janssen Cosmetics


Janssen Cosmetics is a German cosmeceutical skincare brand known for its science-backed products. They combine pharmaceutical-grade active ingredients with luxurious cosmetic formulations to deliver visible and long-lasting results for all skin types. Founded in 1997, the brand is a pioneer in developing cosmeceutical skincare solutions.

SKEYNDOR


Skeyndor is a brand from Spain which started in 1966, and since then it is continuously innovating and setting trends in the cosmetics industry. With rich heritage, enormous background in R&D and ranking of being a No.1 brand in Spain in terms of market share, it is being currently exported to 60 countries across the globe.

MAC


M·A·C Cosmetics, founded in Toronto, Canada, in 1984, is a globally recognized brand known for its professional-grade makeup and innovative artistry. Initially created for makeup artists, it gained popularity for its wide range of colors, inclusive shade ranges, and commitment to diversity.

Aroma Treasures


Aroma Treasures brings nature’s essence to your wellness journey. Our pure essential oils and blends refresh your mind, body, and soul. Each product is crafted to promote deep relaxation and holistic healing.Experience the power of aromatherapy for balance and rejuvenation.

THALGO


Thalgo is a French brand specializing in marine cosmetology, known for its skincare products that harness the power of algae and other marine extracts. Founded in 1964, the company focuses on using marine ingredients like sea minerals, seawater, and algae in their formulations.

Loreal Professionals


L'Oréal Professionnel is a professional hair care brand and a division of L'Oréal, focused on supporting hairdressers and developing the professional hair industry. It offers a range of products and services, including hair color, styling, and haircare, designed for use in salons and for at-home maintenance.
